About Dr. DJ

Dr. DJ is honored to have been selected as the 2021 American Business Woman of the American Business Women's Association (ABWA) and a 2021 Top Ten Business Woman of ABWA. She is a past ABWA Ambassador Steering Committee Representative/Co-Representative and past president of the Douglas County Charter Chapter of ABWA.

Dr. Johnson-Blake serves on several boards, including the Immediate Past Chair of Partners in Education of Douglas County, Inc., and the Beyond the Front Porch board. She is a member of the Junior League of Douglas County and the Douglas County Chamber of Commerce.

Dr. DJ serves as a Douglas County Chamber of Commerce Ambassador and is its 2020 Ambassador of the Year. She is a founding member of the National Council of Negro Women Northwest Georgia Section, a board member of the Georgia Association for Women in Higher Education (GAWHE), serving as its Parliamentarian and is a Georgia Continuing Education Association member.

She is the Vice Chair of the Hospital Authority Board of Douglas County and a board member of the Wellstar Regional Health Board. She has been recognized in the Who's Who in Black Atlanta publication and is an approved subject of biographical record in Who's Who in America. Dr. Johnson-Blake was bestowed the inaugural 2022 EncourageHer award by the Douglas County Chamber of Commerce, among other recognitions.
